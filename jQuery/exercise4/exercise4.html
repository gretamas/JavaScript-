Mojito Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 mint leaves
  • 1/2 ounce simple syrup
  • 2 ounces white rum
  • 3/4 ounce lime juice, freshly squeezed
  • Club soda, to top
  • Garnish: mint sprig
  • Garnish: lime wheel

Negroni Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce gin
  • 1 ounce sweet vermouth
  • 1 ounce Campari
  • Orange twist, for garnish

Bloody Mary Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 ounces vodka
  • 3 ounces tomato juice
  • 1/2 ounce lemon juice
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
  • Celery salt, to taste
  • Ground black pepper, to taste
  • Hot sauce, to taste
  • Celery stalk, for garnish
  • Lemon wedge, for garnish

Margarita Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces tequila
  • 1 ounce lime juice
  • 1 ounce triple sec
  • Salt, for rimming the glass
  • Lime wedge, for garnish

Paloma Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces tequila
  • 1/2 ounce lime juice
  • Pinch of salt
  • Grapefruit soda
  • Grapefruit wedge, for garnish
